Split Fold Training Hub — Section 3.5.3

Without any user command, the sigil for Threadwake manifested in midair, spinning as if caught in its own loop. No resonance source was detected. Witness logs disagree on its emotional signature—some say longing, others vengeance. The loop faded after seven seconds, leaving no trace in the Lattice.
